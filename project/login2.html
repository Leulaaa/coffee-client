GLOBALLY RECOGNIZED TASTES


Arabica

Arabica coffee: a true delight for coffee connoisseurs. Grown at higher altitudes, it boasts exceptional flavor and aroma. With nuanced profiles ranging from fruity to chocolatey, Arabica beans are the preferred choice for specialty coffee. Indulge in the exquisite taste of Arabica and experience the pinnacle of coffee excellence.

Robusta

Robusta coffee: a robust and bold choice for coffee enthusiasts. Grown at lower altitudes, it offers a stronger and more bitter flavor profile. Known for its higher caffeine content and rich crema, Robusta beans add depth and intensity to blends. Embrace the boldness of Robusta and discover a coffee experience with a powerful kick.

Excelsa

Excelsa coffee: a unique and distinct member of the coffee family. It offers a complex flavor profile with hints of tartness and fruity notes. Often considered a subcategory of Liberica, Excelsa beans contribute to blends, adding depth and complexity. Experience the intriguing taste of Excelsa coffee and embark on a journey of flavor exploration.

Liberica

Liberica coffee: a rare and distinctive coffee variety. Known for its robust and bold flavor profile, it offers a unique taste experience. With its large beans and smoky, woody notes, Liberica stands out among other coffee types. Embrace the rarity and richness of Liberica coffee and discover a flavor unlike any other.






LIGHT-ROASTED


Light-roasted coffee: a delicate and nuanced delight. This roast level brings out the coffee's natural flavors and highlights its unique characteristics. With a light brown color and a subtle acidity, it offers a vibrant and crisp taste on the palate. The lighter roast allows the original flavors of the beans to shine through, resulting in a more complex and intricate cup. Perfect for those who appreciate the intricate subtleties of coffee, light-roasted coffee is a delightful choice for a refined and refreshing coffee experience.

MEDIUM-ROASTED


Medium-roasted coffee: a balanced and versatile choice. This roast level strikes a harmonious balance between flavor development and acidity. With a medium brown color and a smooth body, it offers a well-rounded and approachable taste. The medium roast brings out a combination of sweetness, acidity, and complexity, making it a popular option for a wide range of coffee enthusiasts. Whether enjoyed black or with milk, medium-roasted coffee delivers a satisfying and consistent flavor experience. It's the perfect choice for those seeking a balanced cup that showcases the best of both worlds in terms of flavor and aroma.

HARD-ROASTED


Hard-roasted coffee: a bold and intense experience. This roast level pushes the beans to their limits, resulting in a deep, dark brown color and a robust flavor profile. Hard-roasted coffee offers a strong body, pronounced bitterness, and a caramelized taste. The extended roasting process brings out smoky, earthy notes, creating a rich and full-bodied cup. It's the perfect choice for those who prefer a powerful and bold coffee experience that lingers on the palate. Hard-roasted coffee is sure to satisfy those seeking a strong and invigorating brew that packs a punch.

BLEND-ROASTED


Blend-roasted coffee: a masterful combination of flavors. This roast style brings together different coffee varieties to create a unique and well-rounded cup. By blending beans with distinct characteristics, it achieves a balanced acidity, body, and flavor profile. Each blend is carefully crafted to deliver a harmonious taste experience that appeals to a wide range of palates. With blend-roasted coffee, you can enjoy the best of multiple origins in a single cup. Indulge in the complexity and depth that blend-roasted coffee has to offer.
